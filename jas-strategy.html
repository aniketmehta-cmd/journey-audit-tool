Claims & Guardrails — All Creators

Safe to say

  • Temple/hairline recession, M-shaped hairline
  • DHT sensitive follicles ko weak/thin kar sakta hai
  • Genetics is risk/starting point, not destiny
  • Follicles stressed/inactive — not necessarily gone
  • 5–6 month consistency journey
  • Doctor consultation / stage check CTA
  • Shedding in first 2-3 weeks may happen — not failure
  • Minoxidil + Procapil + Biotin Gummies (components)

Do not say

  • "Stop your hairline from receding" (guaranteed)
  • "DHT ko block kiya ja sakta hai" (unless approved)
  • "No side effects" / "koi dryness nahi"
  • "Follicles wapas active ho jayenge" (guaranteed)
  • Exact ingredient quantities without label confirmation
  • Guaranteed regrowth or before/after as universal outcome
  • "India's first FDA approved" without current verification
  • Oral minoxidil in brief unless medically reviewed
Note: All creator scripts require internal review before briefing. BeforeAfter assets require substantiated same-angle proof. Consult medical/regulatory team before any final external use of health claims.
