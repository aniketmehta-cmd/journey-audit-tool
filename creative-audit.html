Connect Google Sheet

What is the Sheet ID?
Open your Google Sheet. Look at the URL in your browser — it looks like:
https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1AbCdEfGhIjKlMnOpQrStUvWxYz1234567890/edit
The bold part between /d/ and /edit is your Sheet ID.
Setup steps:
1. Open the Google Sheet where you want data saved
2. Click Extensions → Apps Script (top menu of the Sheet)
3. Delete any existing code in the editor
4. Click 📋 Copy Script below → paste into the editor
5. Click Save (💾 icon or Cmd+S)
6. Click Deploy → New deployment
7. Click the gear ⚙ next to "Select type" → choose Web app
8. Set "Execute as" → Me · Set "Who has access" → Anyone
9. Click Deploy → click Authorize access → choose your Google account → Allow
10. Copy the Web app URL that appears → paste below
